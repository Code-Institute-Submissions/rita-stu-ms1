Grow Tomatoes from Seed or from Seedlings

Our top-selling seeds come highly recommended by our customers and have been proven to yield the most flavourful fruit, while remaining versatile and easy to grow. These are our own preferred varities too!

Click on the images for more details (including pricing) on each individual fruit. Some varities offer the option to purchase seedlings instead of seeds. Tomato seedlings are very young tomato plants that were planted from seeds. Please note: seedlings are seasonal and may not be available at your time of purchase.

Origin and Provenance of Our Seeds

All heirloom tomato seeds, whether offered or recommended, are organic and free from GMOs. Likewise, all hybrid seed characteristics are derived from GMO-free, organic breeds of tomatoes. Breeder information is provided on the individual product sales pages, and as an insert with every package of seeds purchased.

Provenance is noted for each individual product and is included with each purchase. We also include instructions on how to grow tomatoes from seed, as well as plenty of advice about caring for your new tomato plants.

If you’re unsure which type of tomatos you’d like to grow, don’t forget about our selector tool which was developed especially for moments like this. Simply input your parameters like climate type, the type of planting space you have, the type of soil in your garden, your needs, your preferred yield ... you can even narrow down your choice of taste! Just feed the selector and it will offer a couple of suggestions to get you started.
